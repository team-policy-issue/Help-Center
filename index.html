What type of things aren't allowed on facebook?

  • Nudity or other sexually suggestive content
  • Hate speech, credible threats or direct attacks on an individual or group
  • Content that contains self-harm or excessive violence
  • Fake or impostor profiles
  • Spam

What type of behaviour does facebook identify as abuse?

  • Posting things that don't follow the Facebook Community Standards (e.g. threats, hate speech, graphic violence).
  • Using Facebook to bully, impersonate or harass anyone.
  • Content that contains self-harm or excessive violence
  • Abusing Facebook features (e.g. sending friend requests to many people you don't know). Overusing features could make other people feel uncomfortable or unsafe. As a result, we have limits in place to limit the rate at which you can use features. Learn more about these limits.
